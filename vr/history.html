What is Vr History?

Invented in the 1950s, VR's development has experienced peaks and troughs.
The first VR head-mounted display (HMD) system, The Sword of Damocles, was invented in 1968 by computer scientist Ivan Sutherland and his student Bob Sproull. Meanwhile, the term “virtual reality” was popularised by Jaron Lanier in the 1980s .

Significant Dates of Vr History

1956 – Sensorama, considered to be one of the earliest VR systems, was invented

1968 – The first VR HMD, The Sword of Damocles, was created.

1977 – Developed at MIT, the Aspen Movie Map enabled users to vitake a virtual tour of Aspen, Colorado.
